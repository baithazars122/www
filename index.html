Features

Version-controlled, searchable, and secure

Single source of truth

Balthazar is the digital backbone of your lab - linking prototypes, protocols, results, and metadata in one structured, searchable system. Role-based access supports secure collaboration across teams and sites, while flexible deployment (cloud or on-prem) gives you total control. Its a scalable, secure platform managed for you - so your team moves faster, and no knowledge gets lost along the way.

The secret? Python.

Your experiments, tracked automatically

Balthazar hooks into your existing workflows, tracking every step of your R&D process automatically. Whether youre running physical tests or simulations, it captures data, parameters, and outcomes in real-time. Every prototype gets a digital twin, with version-controlled protocols, scripts, and results. Inputs, outputs, and decisions are traceable and linkedgiving you a living, searchable record of everything your team does.

View and compare in real time

Dashboards for live insights

Balthazar adds a real-time, visual layer to your entire R&D process. Dashboards update live, helping you compare batches, monitor KPIs, and identify champion devices as data comes in. Whether youre validating a model or analyzing trends, you can overlay simulations and experiments in one view - no need to switch tools or write scripts to plot your data.

AI that lives in your world

ML-powered R&D

Integrate machine learning directly into your experimental pipeline - from design iteration to real-time execution. Use past results to suggest new prototype designs or optimize fabrication recipes. Train and deploy models for live data acquisition or closed-loop optimization, automatically tuning parameters as experiments run. Stay tuned for our LLM-powered Labthazar, a copilot grounded in your labs context.

Share, comment, and stay in sync

Collaborate real-time from your browser

Balthazar is built for real-time collaboration, with shared workspaces that give teams live access to results, protocols, and reusable workflows. Comments and @mentions keep communication in context without the need to switch tools. The interface is fast, focused, and intuitive, making complex workflows easy to execute - both for engineers and operators.

Blends right in

Up and running, instantly

Use Python to orchestrate experiments, integrate with third-party tools, or run ML algorithms on top of your results. Connect to your Git and use existing code, or start fresh with an integrated repo in Balthazar. Your plots appear automatically, and theres no need to change how or where you store your data.
